Scene &  to

static

Copies the assignments from one scene to another.

This copies the assignments (runs, stops) from one scene to another. If there were any stops in the "to" scene, they are destoyed.

Warning
This method assumes that the order of schedules in the "from" scene matches the order of schedules in the "to" scene. It iterates over the schedules of the "from" scene and the schedules of the "to" scene and copies the assignments using the SceneCloner::cloneScheduleAssignments method. The state of first schedule of "from" is copied to the first schedule of "to", of the second - to the second schedule of "to" etc. If the schedules list of "from" and "to" scenes don't match each other, the behavior is undefined.

Copies the hollow schedules from one scene to another.

This copies the hollow copies of the schedules from one scene to another. The order of schedules is preserved, i.e. the iteration over the list of schedules in the "to" scene will process the copies in the same order as their corresponding originals were processed in the "from" scene No assignments are copied so all schedules are empty. If there were any schedules in the "to" scene, they are destoyed.

Copies the order assignments from one schedule to another.

This copies the order assignments (runs, stops) from one schedule to another. This doesn't copy the order assignments. The sequence order of the runs and stops is preserved. If there were any stops assigned to the "to" schedule, they are destroyed.
